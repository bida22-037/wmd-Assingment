The Warner Bros Brothers

The Warner Bros brothers, Harry Warner (1881–1958) and Albert Warner (1884–1967), were the co-founders of Warner Bros. Entertainment Inc., one of the largest and most influential film studios in Hollywood. They played a significant role in the development of the American film industry and contributed to the production of numerous iconic movies.
